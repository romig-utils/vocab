Plaintext Vocab Practice

List 8 | Single

  1. Anachronistic: outdated, obsolete, old-fashioned
  2. Aberration: irregularity, deviation, abnormality
  3. Brusque: harsh, abrupt, curt, gruff
  4. Capacious: large in capacity; roomy, extensive
  5. Deleterious: harmful to living things; toxic, lethal
  6. Evanescent: tending to vanish like vapor; fleeting
  7. Florid: elaborately ornamented; flamboyant, fancy
  8. Fortitude: strength of mind that enables one to endure adversity with courage; determination, courage, resilience
  9. Impute: to give credit, assign, attribute
  10. Opulent: superior in quality, magnificent
  11. Perfidious: tending to betray; disloyal, deceitful
  12. Prosaic: lacking imagination; ordinary, plain
  13. Rancorous: showing deep-seated resentment, bitter, unforgiving
  14. Superfluous: more than is needed, desired, or required
  15. Surreptitious: marked by quiet, caution, and secrecy; covert
